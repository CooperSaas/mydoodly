Questions

Everything you need to know

Does the drawing have to be detailed?+
Absolutely not. The messier and more specific to your child, the better. Wobbly lines, mystery creatures, characters with six legs — these make the most unique Doodles.
How long does it take?+
1 to 2 weeks from payment confirmation. An approval photo is sent before shipping so you see your Doodle before it leaves Bali.
What is the heart pocket?+
Every Doodle has a tiny hidden pocket sewn into its back containing a small felt heart. You choose the heart colour when you order. It's a secret your child will discover when they explore their new Doodle.
What is on the birth certificate?+
The birth certificate card is personalised with the Doodle's name (the one you give it when ordering), your child's name as the creator, and the date it was born. It comes inside the gift box.
Can I request specific colours?+
Yes. The order form has a colour preference field. If your child's drawing is already coloured, we follow those colours as closely as possible.
Where do you ship?+
Across Indonesia. Shipping is charged at cost depending on your location. We use J&T, SiCepat, or JNE. Tracking is provided when your Doodle ships.
What if the Doodle doesn't match?+
We send an approval photo before shipping. If something is not right, we will fix it. On first orders we offer a remake for genuine mismatches.
Who makes the Doodles?+
One person — the founder, working from a studio in Bali. Every Doodle is made by the same hands. When you submit your order, you are talking directly to the maker.
